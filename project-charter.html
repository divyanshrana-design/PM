Project Charter

The single document that decides if your project is worth doing. Fill this in once. Get board or LEO mentor sign off. Keep it to one page.

Project name: [Autumn Fundraiser 2026] Date: [dd/mm/yyyy] Version: 1.0

1. The problem or opportunity

Why does this project exist? Describe the situation in plain English. Two or three sentences maximum.

[We currently raise about €8,000 a year through one annual event. Our grant renewals depend on showing €15,000 in community income by December 2026. We need a structured fundraising push this autumn to close the gap.]

2. The goal

One clear, measurable outcome. If you cannot measure it, you cannot deliver it.

[Raise €15,000 in community donations between September and December 2026, with at least 300 unique donors.]

3. In scope

  • [Launch event at a Galway venue in late October]
  • [Online donation campaign across Instagram, LinkedIn and email]
  • [Thank you packs for donors over €100]

Out of scope

  • [Corporate sponsorship outreach, handled separately in Q1 2027]
  • [Merchandise sales]

Risk Likelihood Impact Mitigation

[Key volunteer falls ill] [Medium] [Medium] [Two named backups per role, shared document system]
[Venue cancellation] [Low] [High] [Written contract, backup venue identified, 7 day notice clause]
