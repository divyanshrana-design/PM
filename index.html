The 5 phases

Every project, from a market stall launch to a new grant programme, moves through these.

Initiation

Decide if the project is worth doing. Write a one page charter.

Planning

Break it down, schedule it, spot the risks, agree how you'll communicate.

Execution

Do the work. Unblock the team. Celebrate the small wins.

Monitor & Control

Check progress, adjust scope, catch problems early.

Closing

Hand over, write down the lessons, thank the team.
