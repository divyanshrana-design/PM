Quick answers

Before you ask…

I'm not in Ireland, can we still work together?

Yes, remotely. Most of my client work is via Zoom and Teams anyway. That said, the PM Éire guide is written from and for the Irish context (LEO, charity regulator, GDPR), so it'll be most immediately useful to teams here.

What does an engagement typically look like?

It starts with a free 20 minute call. If there's a fit, I send a short scoping doc with a fixed fee and a clear deliverable. Most engagements run for 1 to 4 weeks; fractional PM work is ongoing.

Are you available for talks and workshops?

Very much so. I regularly run sessions at LEO events, charity forums and company offsites. The "AI in 60 minutes for small Irish teams" workshop is the most requested.

Will my conversation be confidential?

Always. I won't share names or specifics without written permission. If I ever want to write a case study, you'll see and approve it first.

Let's get your projects moving.

Drop me a line. Even if we don't end up working together, I'll point you toward something useful.
