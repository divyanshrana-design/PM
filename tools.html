Software

The best PM tools for Irish SMEs & charities

All have AI built in. All have free trials or free tiers. Prices shown are approximate per user, per month.

Best overall for SMEs

ClickUp

Free tier · Paid from ~€7/user/month

A do everything platform that grows with you. 35+ "ClickApps" to turn features on and off as you need them.

  • Tasks, docs, goals and dashboards in one
  • ClickUp Brain (AI) for summaries and auto subtasks
  • Excellent free tier for small teams
  • Templates library to start fast
Best for: Teams of 4 to 20 who want one tool to rule them all.
Best value for money

Zoho Projects

From ~€4/user/month

Powerful, affordable and underrated. Especially strong if you already use other Zoho tools (CRM, Books, etc.).

  • Built in chat, no separate Slack needed
  • Zia AI assistant for predictions
  • Subtasks, recurring tasks, budgeting
  • Plays nicely with Zoho Books for finance tracking
Best for: Budget conscious SMEs and Irish charities.

Notion

Free · Paid from ~€9/user/month

Wikis, docs, tasks and databases in one. Brilliant if your team needs a knowledge base as much as a task tracker.

  • Drag and drop blocks to build any view
  • Timeline, calendar, kanban, database
  • Notion AI for writing and Q&A
  • Generous free tier for tiny teams
Best for: Writers, agencies, and teams that value documentation.

monday.com

From ~€10/user/month

Visually gorgeous dashboards. Great for teams who like bright, clear "at a glance" views of everything.

  • Highly visual boards and timelines
  • AI blocks you drop onto any board
  • Strong automation builder
  • Good for cross functional work
Best for: Teams where the owner/lead is visual and reports to a board.

Trello

Free tier · Paid from ~€5/user/month

The simplest kanban tool on the internet. If you need "to do, doing, done" and nothing more, start here.

  • Learn it in 5 minutes, literally
  • Excellent free tier for solo/small teams
  • Atlassian Intelligence built in
  • Works great on mobile
Best for: Solo operators, charities just starting, event coordination.

Asana

Free tier · Paid from ~€11/user/month

The polished middle ground. Clean UI, strong timeline view, well liked by marketing and ops teams.

  • Excellent Gantt style timeline
  • Asana Intelligence (AI) across the app
  • Goals feature ties tasks to outcomes
  • Free tier covers up to 10 users
Best for: Marketing teams, client service, cross team coordination.

Free templates

Ready to use templates for your next project

Eight battle tested templates used by real Irish small businesses and charities. Copy them into Google Docs, Sheets or Notion, change the pink bits, and you are ready to go.

Initiation

One page Project Charter

The single document that decides if your project is worth doing. Fits on one A4 page so your board, LEO mentor or funder can actually read it.

  • Problem statement and outcome
  • In scope vs. out of scope list
  • Budget range and top 3 risks
  • Stakeholder sign off block

Work Breakdown Structure

Turn a vague project ("launch the new website") into a plan you can actually deliver. Three levels of detail, colour coded by phase.

  • Phase, deliverable and task rows
  • Owner, due date and RAG status columns
  • 25 prefilled tasks across all 5 phases
  • Worked example: charity autumn fundraiser

Planning

SME Risk Register

Most small teams "do risk" in their heads. That fails the second someone leaves. This sheet scores every risk on likelihood × impact and tells you what to actually do about it.

  • 1 to 5 likelihood times impact scoring
  • Auto coloured high, medium, low heat cells
  • 15 typical Irish SME risks prepopulated
  • Grouped by people, money, suppliers, tech, external

Monitoring

Weekly Status Update

The 5 bullet Friday update that keeps funders, boards and clients calm without eating your whole afternoon. Paste it into email, Slack or Teams.

  • RAG status for schedule, budget, scope, team
  • Done this week and doing next week sections
  • Blockers and decisions needed
  • Budget snapshot and key numbers vs. targets
  • AI prompt to auto draft it from your notes

Who do you actually need to keep happy? Plot every stakeholder on a power × interest grid and get a clear communication plan for each quadrant.

  • 2 by 2 power vs. interest matrix

Lessons Learned Log

The 30 minute session at the end of a project that pays for itself ten times over on the next one. Keeps the good, fixes the bad, and writes it down so the next team benefits.

The smallest AI policy your team actually needs. Written for a 4 to 20 person Irish team. Covers GDPR, EU AI Act basics and the tools your team can and cannot use with client data.

How to use these templates

Each template opens in a new tab, styled exactly like a Google Doc or Google Sheet. Hit the Print or Save PDF button in the top right to save a copy, or simply copy the content into your own Google Workspace account and edit freely. Every placeholder in pink is an area for you to personalise.
